Programs

What does Unimatch Nexus Foundation do?

We support young Kenyans through scholarships, mentorship, digital skills training, Girls in STEM, rural outreach, entrepreneurship support, and partnerships that expand education access.

Where do your programs operate?

Our work is Kenya-focused and grows through school, community, donor, and partner networks. Program activity may vary by county depending on available resources and local partnerships.

How do you choose which programs to run?

We prioritize practical needs that affect education access and youth opportunity: school retention, mentorship, digital readiness, STEM exposure, community outreach, and employability.

Donations & Payments

Which payment methods do you accept?

The site supports M-Pesa, bank transfer, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Google Pay. Some card and wallet options require the final payment gateway credentials before live checkout can be completed.

Can I choose the program my donation supports?

Yes. The donation form lets you select a program such as Scholarship Fund, Digital Skills Lab, Girls in STEM, Rural Outreach, Entrepreneurship Hub, Youth Mentorship, or General Donation.

Will I receive confirmation of my donation?

For M-Pesa, you should receive confirmation through Safaricom when payment is completed. For bank, card, or wallet donations, confirmation depends on the relevant provider and foundation reconciliation.

Can I request a refund for a mistaken donation?

Contact us quickly with the transaction reference, donor name, amount, and payment method. Refunds are reviewed case by case and may depend on provider rules and whether funds have already been committed to program work.

Student Support

How can a student apply for support?

Students, guardians, or schools can contact us through the website contact form. We may request academic details, school information, need assessment, guardian contacts, and supporting documents.

Does applying guarantee sponsorship?

No. Applications are reviewed based on need, program fit, available funds, partner criteria, and verification. We try to help responsibly, but resources are limited.

Do you support only school fees?

No. Support can include mentorship, learning materials, digital skills, STEM exposure, outreach, exam preparation, and career guidance depending on the program and available resources.

Partnerships

How can an organization partner with you?

Use the Partner With Us form on the website. Tell us your organization, contact person, partnership type, and the area you want to support. We will review and follow up.

What partnership options are available?

Organizations can support scholarships, digital labs, mentorship days, STEM clubs, rural outreach, entrepreneurship training, media storytelling, volunteering, and in-kind resources.

Careers & Volunteers

Do you have open jobs?

Openings change as programs grow. Visit the Careers page to see role pathways and application guidance, or email your CV and area of interest to the careers team.

Can I volunteer as a mentor or trainer?

Yes. Volunteers may support mentorship, digital training, career talks, field activities, resource mobilization, or professional advice. Roles involving students may require screening and safeguarding commitments.

Press & Media

Can media teams cover your work?

Yes. Media teams can contact us for approved field visits, interviews, photos, program updates, and verified impact stories.

May I use your photos or stories?

Please request permission before using foundation photos, student stories, brand materials, or program images. We protect the dignity and privacy of the young people and communities we serve.
