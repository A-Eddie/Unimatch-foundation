Build work that changes futures.

Join a mission-driven team helping young Kenyans access education, mentorship, digital skills, STEM opportunities, rural outreach, and entrepreneurship support.

Where You Can Serve

Work close to people, purpose, and possibility.

Our team combines office strategy, field delivery, digital learning, and one-on-one mentorship. Every role is expected to protect dignity, act with integrity, and keep learners at the center.

Program Strategy

Collaborate with colleagues to plan scholarship support, partnerships, donor reporting, and measurable program delivery.

Field Outreach

Work directly with schools and communities to identify needs, coordinate activities, and keep support grounded in real life.

Digital Learning

Help learners gain practical technology confidence through guided labs, online safety, research skills, and creative projects.

Mentorship

Support students as they set goals, make informed choices, build confidence, and prepare for the next stage of life.

How We Work

We are looking for people with skill and heart.

Talent matters, but so does character. The strongest candidates bring discipline, humility, initiative, and deep respect for the communities we serve.

01

Serve With Dignity

We never reduce people to need. We listen carefully, communicate respectfully, and design support that preserves agency.

02

Deliver With Excellence

We value clear planning, honest reporting, careful follow-up, and work that donors, schools, students, and partners can trust.

03

Learn and Improve

We test ideas, measure outcomes, welcome feedback, and keep improving how we support young people across Kenya.

Career Pathways

Roles we regularly build around.

Current openings may change, but these are the kinds of people we look for as our programs grow.

ProgramsFull-time / Project-based

Program Officer

Coordinate school visits, learner follow-up, mentorship sessions, partner activities, and program reporting.

  • Strong field coordination and communication skills.
  • Experience with education, youth work, or community programs.
  • Comfortable with data, reports, and stakeholder follow-up.
TechnologyTrainer / Facilitator

Digital Skills Trainer

Lead practical digital literacy sessions and help students build confidence using technology for learning and opportunity.

  • Strong computer, internet, and presentation skills.
  • Patient teaching style for beginners.
  • Ability to manage classroom energy and learning outcomes.
PartnershipsDonor Relations

Partnerships Associate

Build relationships with companies, schools, NGOs, media partners, and donors who want to support meaningful education work.

  • Excellent writing, follow-up, and presentation ability.
  • Confidence preparing proposals and impact updates.
  • Strong relationship management and professionalism.
MentorshipVolunteer / Part-time

Youth Mentor

Guide students through academic confidence, career exploration, life skills, goal-setting, and transitions after school.

  • Strong listening skills and emotional maturity.
  • Commitment to safeguarding and positive youth development.
  • Ability to show up consistently and communicate clearly.
