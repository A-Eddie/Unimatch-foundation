Privacy Policy

How we protect your information.

Unimatch Nexus Foundation collects only the information needed to serve donors, partners, students, volunteers, media contacts, and communities with care and accountability.

Effective date: June 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you donate, contact us, submit a partnership request, volunteer, apply for support, subscribe to updates, or interact with our programs.

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, organization, and role.
  • Donation details such as amount, selected program, payment method, pledge note, and payment reference.
  • Program information submitted by students, guardians, schools, or partners when requesting support.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, and basic website usage data where analytics are enabled.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to process donations, respond to messages, coordinate programs, manage partnerships, send receipts or updates, improve our services, and meet legal, financial, and reporting obligations.

3. Payments and Donor Data

Payments may be processed through M-Pesa, banks, card processors, PayPal, Google Pay, or other approved providers. We do not intentionally store full card numbers, card security codes, or payment passwords on this website. Payment providers may collect and process payment information under their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers, payment processors, auditors, legal advisers, program partners, or authorities where required to operate responsibly, verify transactions, protect users, or comply with the law.

5. Data Protection and Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. We keep information only as long as needed for program delivery, donor accountability, legal compliance, financial records, dispute handling, and reporting.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or review of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable laws and legitimate record-keeping requirements.

7. Children and Students

Some programs support minors and students. Where appropriate, we work with guardians, schools, or authorized partners and handle student information with additional care, dignity, and confidentiality.
